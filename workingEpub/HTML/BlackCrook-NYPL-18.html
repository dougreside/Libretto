Scene 7th
Subterranean gallery of emerald + crystal stalactites (in 1)
Music() at opening of scene, characteristic march--Grand procession of Amphibea and Gnomes bearing in their arms and upon their heads salvers and shells and quaint vases filled with gold and jewels, they are followed by Amazons in armor, led by Stalacta.
