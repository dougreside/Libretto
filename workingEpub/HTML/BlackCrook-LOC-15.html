Scene IV.--
A burning forest. A grand and imposing conflagation scene (in 2.) Lights down. Music at opening of scene. Loud crackling noise (torpedoes) and red fire on both sides. Rodolphe and Amina enter L.H. 2d ent. ; as they attempt to escape they are driven back from R. and L. by fiends with flaming torches.
Am.
[In agony.]
I burn! I suffocate!
Rod.
Courage! Courage! The Ring! The Ring!
[He kisses the ring; gong sounds; a rock opens in L.H. flat, disclosing a grotto of silver stalactites; they quickly enter ; Hertzog and the fiends spring forward, when Stalacta steps forth from the opening, holding aloft a glittering cross; Hertzog and the fiends shrink back and run off ; Tableaux closed in.
