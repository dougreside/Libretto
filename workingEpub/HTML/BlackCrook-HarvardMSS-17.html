Scene 6th
Pandemonium. (in 1st cut of 3) Zamiel in council, seated on an illuminated throne of skulls and flame. (C. at back) To R + L of centre lesser thrones. That on the R. occupied by
Redglare
the recording demon, with flaming ink-horn, pen of fire and open book, that on the L. by a secretary, writing. At the foot of the central throne two dwarf demon pages with wands in attendance.
Music at opening
Fiends discovered in a chorus of demoniacal yells and fiendish laughter, dancing around a flaming chasm. (C.) Music at opening of scene.
After the action is continued a brief time, Zamiel waves his sceptre. Demons separate and form R + L.
Zamiel
'Tis well! Let silence reign awhile. How stands the record of the dying year? Has every seed brought sinful fruit? Is all the harvest gathered in--is every bond fulfilled?
Redglare
All, all save one.
Zamiel
Who plays the laggard?
Redglare
One who sought to rival thy great power. Hertzog, the Black Crook.
All the Fiends
Ho, ho, go.

If when the brazen tongue of clamorous time--now trembling on the midnights verge--proclaims the appointed hour, the wail of the fresh soul, by him betrayed, breaks on the air of Hell, let him be summoned.
