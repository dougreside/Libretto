Scene 4th
A burning forest--a grand and comprehensive conflagation (in 2)
painted
rock, covered with creeping vines and led by dwarfed trees. (C)
with sliding panel in L.H. flat
Music--at opening of scene loud crackling noise, (torpedoes) and strong red light on all sides. Rodolphe and Amina Enter L.H.2nd Ent. as they attempt to escape at the different entrances they are driven back by fiends with flaming torches.
Amina
(In agony)
I burn, I suffocate!
Rodolphe
Courage! Courage! The Ring! The Ring!
(He kisses the ring) Gong sounds, the rock opens disclosing a stalactite grotto, they quickly enter. Hertzog and the fiends spring forward, when Stalacta steps forth holding aloft a glittering cross. Hertzog and fiends shrink back.
