Scene VIII.--
  • Transformation Polka
    Sheet Music
[Music at opening.] An elaborate mechanical and scenical construction of the Realms of Stalacta, occupying the entire stage. This scene must be of gradually developing and culminating beauty, introducing, during its various transformations, Stalacta, the entire host of fairies, sprites, water nymphs, amphibea, gnomes, etc., bearing treasure. Rodolphe and Amina, Greppo and Carline. Calcium lights, brilliant fires and slow curtain. Draphin car descends.
