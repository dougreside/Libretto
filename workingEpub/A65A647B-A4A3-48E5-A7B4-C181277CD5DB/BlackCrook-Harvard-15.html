Scene 4th
Burning forest--a grand & comprehensive conflagation scene, in 2) Lights down. Music() at opening of Scene. Loud crackling noise (torpedoes) and red fire on both sides. Rodolphe and Amina Enter L.H.2nd Ent. as they attempt to escape they are driven back from R. + L. by fiends with flaming torches.
Amina
(In agony)
I burn! I suffocate!
Rodolphe
Courage, courage. The Ring! The Ring!
(He kisses the ring--Gong sounds, a rock opens in L.H. flat disclosing a grotto of silver Stalactites. They quickly enter. Hertzog and the fiends spring forward, when Stalacta steps forward from the opening, holding aloft a glittering cross. Hertzog & the Fiends shrink back. Tableaux
